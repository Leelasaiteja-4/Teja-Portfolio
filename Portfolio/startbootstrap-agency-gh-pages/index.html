About -me

I am Leela Sai Teja , an enthusiastic interior designer with a passion for creating functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces. With a strong foundation in industry-leading design tools such as SketchUp, V-Ray, and Revit Architecture, I bring creative concepts to life with precision and detail. My approach combines technical expertise with a keen eye for design, ensuring that every project is not only visually stunning but also meets the specific needs of each client.

  • SketchUp has allowed me to create detailed and accurate 3D models, giving life to design concepts with ease and flexibility.
  • V-Ray empowers me to produce photorealistic renderings, ensuring that the final vision is both aesthetically appealing and true to life.
  • Revit Architecture has enhanced my understanding of building information modeling (BIM), enabling me to work with precision and collaborate effectively on complex architectural projects.

    • My strengths lie in my keen attention to detail, problem-solving skills, and the ability to blend creativity with technical knowledge. These courses have shaped my approach to design, making me confident in delivering high-quality work that balances beauty and functionality
